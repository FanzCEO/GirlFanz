GirlFanz brand kit (v1)

Core palette (dark-first UI)
	•	GF Pink (Primary): #FF2E7E — electric magenta for CTAs/links
	•	Royal Violet (Secondary): #6C2BD9 — depth + luxe for highlights
	•	Cyber Cyan (Accent): #00E5FF — hover states, micro-accents
	•	Ink (Bg): #0A0A0B — main background
	•	Graphite (Surface): #121214 — cards/modals
	•	Snow (Text on dark): #F5F7FA — primary copy
	•	Rose Smoke (Muted text): #C7BBD3 — labels/secondary
	•	Success: #10B981 — payouts/green lights
	•	Warning: #F59E0B
	•	Error: #EF4444

Signature gradients
	•	Heatwave: linear-gradient(135deg, #FF2E7E 0%, #6C2BD9 50%, #00E5FF 100%)
	•	Afterglow (CTA): linear-gradient(90deg, #FF2E7E, #6C2BD9)
	•	Nightdrive (Background hero wash): radial-gradient(1200px 600px at 70% -10%, #6C2BD922 0%, #000 60%)

Typography
	•	Display: Space Grotesk / Sora / Plus Jakarta Sans (tight tracking, bold)
	•	Body: Inter / General Sans (crisp and legible)

UI mood
	•	Rounded-but-sharp: 10–12px radius, glassy overlays (2–4% white on dark), soft ambient glows in GF Pink/Cyber Cyan.
	•	Micro-motion: 120–180ms ease-out, slight parallax on hero, hover glow on CTAs.

Component cues
	•	Primary button: GF Pink → Afterglow gradient on hover; soft outer glow 0 0 24px #FF2E7E66.
	•	Secondary button: Royal Violet outline, text Snow; hover fill #6C2BD9 12% with Cyan underline animation.
	•	Tabs/pills: Graphite base; active pill GF Pink with Cyan 2px underline.
	•	Cards: Graphite with 1px border #2A2A2E; top edge accent glow (GF Pink at 20% opacity).
	•	Badges: Creator tiers in Violet/Cyan duotones; payouts in Success.

Accessibility guardrails
	•	Keep GF Pink on Graphite/Ink (contrast passes for CTA text ≥ 4.5:1 using Snow text).
	•	Use Snow for primary text, Rose Smoke for secondary (never under 14px).
	•	Don’t place Cyan text on Pink (insufficient contrast); use Cyan as accent lines/icons.

Brand shapes & media
	•	Abstract ribbons/light trails in Pink→Violet→Cyan.
	•	Photo treatment: subtle violet-cyan duotone overlay (multiply at ~12–18%) to unify UGC thumbnails.
